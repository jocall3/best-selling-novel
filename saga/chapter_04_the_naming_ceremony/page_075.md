### Document ID: FFN-001-REV-B
### Date of Compilation: Cycle 73, Day 12, Post-Unification Era

---

**Foreword:**
The following document serves as a historical record of the numerous appellations considered and ultimately rejected during the foundational naming ceremony for what would eventually become the esteemed "Nexus of Enlightenment." While the process was arduous, and at times, bewildering, it is crucial to remember the spirit of innovation and the boundless, if occasionally misguided, creativity that permeated those early deliberations. This compendium is presented not as a testament to failure, but as a humorous, albeit lengthy, reminder of the path taken to achieve our current, dignified nomenclature.

**Concluding Remark:**
It is with profound relief that the Grand Council ultimately settled upon "Nexus of Enlightenment," a name that, in retrospect, seems so utterly obvious and fitting. This document stands as a testament to the journey, however circuitous, towards that singular, perfect designation. May future generations appreciate the brevity of our current name, and perhaps, find amusement in the verbose alternatives that were, thankfully, left behind.
