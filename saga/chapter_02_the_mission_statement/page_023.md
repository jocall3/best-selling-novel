## I. Campaign Overview: "The Rhombus and the Rational"

This campaign aims to generate initial buzz and philosophical curiosity around the core themes of the SAGA narrativeâ€”the pursuit of absolute, ineffable truth (represented by the Rhombus) and the flawed, instinctual nature of the seeker (represented by Professor Diggles).

**Target Audience:** Philosophically inclined early adopters, fans of abstract science fiction, and viewers drawn to surreal, high-concept short-form content.

**Core Message:** Some truths cannot be sniffed, poked, or buried. They must simply be *seen*.

**Format:** A series of three (3) 15-second, high-production-value online video ads (optimized for social media feeds).

---

## II. Character & Prop Specification

**Character:** **Professor Diggles.** A live-action, highly trained European Badger (must appear scholarly, perhaps wearing a tiny, slightly askew tweed vest). Must exhibit intense, focused, yet ultimately futile curiosity.

**Prop:** **The Perfect Rhombus.** A flawless, glowing, golden-white geometric solid. It emits a soft, non-blinding light and appears mathematically impossible in its perfection. It should hover slightly above the ground or a pedestal.
