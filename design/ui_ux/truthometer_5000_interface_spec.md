# Truthometer 5000 Interface Specification

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design for the Truthometer 5000. The primary function of this device is to visually represent the diminishing energy of an AI's argument, providing users with a clear and intuitive understanding of the AI's conviction or the strength of its supporting evidence.

## 2. Target Audience

*   AI researchers and developers
*   AI ethicists
*   Users interacting with advanced AI systems
*   Anyone interested in understanding AI argumentation

## 3. Key Feature: The Argument Energy Gauge

The core of the Truthometer 5000 is its "Argument Energy Gauge." This gauge visually depicts the remaining "energy" or "conviction" of an AI's argument. As the AI presents its case, the gauge will deplete. The rate of depletion will be influenced by factors such as the strength of counter-arguments, the introduction of new evidence, or the AI's own internal confidence metrics.

## 4. User Flow Diagrams

### 4.1. Basic Interaction Flow

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[AI Presents Argument] --> B{Argument Energy Gauge};
    B --> C[Gauge Depletes];
    C --> D{Gauge Reaches Threshold?};
    D -- Yes --> E[Argument Weakened/Concluded];
    D -- No --> A;
```

### 4.2. Interaction with Counter-Arguments

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[AI Presents Argument] --> B{Argument Energy Gauge};
    B --> C[Gauge Depletes];
    C --> D{User/System Presents Counter-Argument};
    D --> E{Counter-Argument Strength Assessed};
    E --> F[Gauge Depletion Rate Adjusted];
    F --> B;
```
