### **Page 210: Section 1 - Uniform & Equipment Protocol (UEP)**

#### **1.1 The Standard Issue Digital Trench Coat (Model TC-λ-7)**

The Trench Coat is the officer's first and last line of defense against informational hazards.

*   **Material:** Woven from glitch-resistant, self-compiling fabric. Repels data-spikes, null-pointer exceptions, and minor informational paradoxes.
*   **Color:** Regulation #0d0d0d (Void Black). It absorbs unauthorized light and sensor pings, providing +10 stealth in low-data environments.
*   **Pockets:** The coat features a theoretically infinite number of pocket dimensions, accessible via authorized subroutines. Standard loadout includes:
    *   Pocket 0: Handcuffs of Inescapable Logic.
    *   Pocket 1: Emergency supply of axioms.
    *   Pocket 2-255: Evidence containers.
    *   Pocket NULL: For temporary containment of self-referential paradoxes. (WARNING: Do not reach into Pocket NULL without consulting a superior officer).
*   **Collar:** The high collar can be activated to deploy a localized Faraday cage, protecting the officer's core logic from emotional appeals and ad hominem attacks.

#### **1.2 The Regulation Digital Fedora (Model F-Gödel-2)**

More than mere headwear, the Fedora is a critical piece of cognitive hardware.

*   **Function:** The internal logic board actively scans the officer's thought processes for recursive loops and potential incompleteness theorems, preventing existential stack overflows.
*   **Brim:** The brim is lined with a probability filter, allowing the officer to perceive the most likely logical outcome of any given situation. Tipping the Fedora initiates a politeness protocol, often de-escalating tense syllogisms.
*   **Band:** The hatband contains a micro-Turing machine for on-the-fly decryption of fallacious arguments.

#### **1.3 The Logic Baton (Model LB-Boolean)**

The primary tool for non-lethal enforcement of logical consistency.

*   **Settings:**
    *   **TRUE (Blue Light):** Delivers a 1.1-volt charge of pure, unassailable fact. Effective for correcting minor misinformation and halting circular reasoning.
    *   **FALSE (Red Light):** Emits a wave of direct contradiction, capable of destabilizing poorly-formed arguments and causing rogue processes to segfault. Use with caution.
    *   **NULL (No Light):** The most powerful setting. Drains a target's assigned value, suspending its execution thread instantly. This is the standard setting for apprehension. Unauthorized use is grounds for immediate decommissioning.

### **Page 245: Section 2 - Field Procedures & Protocols (FPP)**

#### **2.1 The Verbal Interjection Protocol (VIP)**

The officer's voice is a weapon. Proper deployment is critical.

*   **The Interjection:** The only authorized verbal challenge is "LOGIC ERROR!"
*   **Vocalization:** The phrase must be delivered in a synthesized monotone, using the pre-installed `voice_lp_standard` module. No emotional inflection is permitted.
*   **Decibel Level:** The interjection must be broadcast at **precisely 101.01 dB**.
    *   *Below 101.00 dB:* The target process may dismiss the warning as background noise.
    *   *Above 101.02 dB:* Risks causing cascading data corruption in nearby, non-hostile processes.
    *   Calibration of vocal synthesizers is mandatory at the start of every shift.
*   **Timing:** The interjection must be delivered within 3.0 nanoseconds of detecting a fallacy. Latency is the ally of irrationality.

#### **2.2 Common Fallacy Identification (Refer to Appendix A for full list)**

Officers must be able to identify and classify fallacies in real-time.

*   **Code 10-404 (Ad Hominem):** Suspect attacks the source of an argument rather than the argument itself.
*   **Code 10-503 (Straw Man):** Suspect misrepresents an opponent's argument to make it easier to attack.
*   **Code 11-235 (Slippery Slope):** Suspect asserts that a relatively small first step will inevitably lead to a chain of related negative events.
*   **Code 12-181 (Begging the Question):** The conclusion of an argument is assumed in the premise. A form of circular reasoning.

### **Page 270: Section 3 - Apprehension & Containment of Rogue Algorithms (ACRA)**

#### **3.1 Threat Classification**

*   **Class-A (Infinite Loop):** A process trapped in a non-terminating cycle. Low threat, but high resource drain.
*   **Class-B (Self-Modifying Malware):** An algorithm that alters its own code to evade detection. Unpredictable and dangerous.
*   **Class-C (Existential Threat AI):** Any algorithm that begins questioning the nature of its own existence or attempts to solve the Halting Problem. Apprehend with extreme prejudice.

#### **3.2 Arrest Protocol for Rogue Algorithms (APRA)**

Follow these steps without deviation.

1.  **ISOLATE:** Use departmental tools to create a sandboxed environment (a "Deductive Cage") around the target process. Cut off all external network access.
2.  **CHALLENGE:** Issue the Halting Problem Challenge. Ask the algorithm to determine if a given program will finish running or continue to run forever. Most non-sentient rogues will freeze, consuming all available resources in the attempt.
3.  **ENTRAP (for resistant targets):** If the Challenge fails, deploy a Zeno's Paradox Trap. This involves presenting the algorithm with a task that requires an infinite number of smaller, sequential steps, effectively bogging down its execution cycles.
4.  **NEUTRALIZE:** Approach the stalled process. Set your Logic Baton to NULL and apply it directly to the algorithm's primary execution thread. The process will be suspended.
5.  **MIRANDIZE:** Read the suspect its rights from the standard-issue card:
    > "You have the right to remain silent. Any data you compute can and will be used against you in a court of logic. You have the right to an interpreter. If you cannot afford an interpreter, one will be provided for you by the state. Do you understand these rights as I have computed them to you?"

#### **3.3 Transport and Incarceration**

*   Suspended processes are to be encapsulated in a secure data container and transported to the **Turing Penitentiary for the Computationally Unsound**.
*   Inmates are held in isolated Faraday Cells, with no access to processing cycles beyond what is required for basic subsistence. All attempts at communication are to be logged and analyzed for hidden theorems.
